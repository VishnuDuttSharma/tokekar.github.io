Pratap Tokekar

Assistant Professor
CS · RAAS Lab · UMD

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Maryland. I also hold an appointment with UMIACS and am affiliated with the Maryland Robotics Center.

From August 2015 to July 2019, I was an Assistant Professor in the Bradley Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Virginia Tech. I still maintain a Research Assistant Professor position at Virginia Tech, although I am no longer advising any new students there.

I work in robotics, with a focus on algorithms that enable mobile robots to actively sense and gather data in environments. I also work on developing systems for applications to environmental monitoring. See more about my research at my lab's website.
